Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free trial?

Our 14-day free trial gives you full access to all features of the Professional plan. No credit card required. You can downgrade to a free limited plan or upgrade at any time.

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When you upgrade, you'll get immediate access to the new features. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?

We offer a 25% discount for registered nonprofits. Please contact our sales team with proof of your nonprofit status to get this discount applied to your account.

How does billing work?

We bill monthly or annually in advance. Annual plans offer a 20% discount. You can cancel anytime before your next billing cycle.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to help you choose the right plan for your needs.
