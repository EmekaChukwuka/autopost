Our Story

How a team of marketers and engineers built the social media automation tool they wished existed

Why We Built AutoPost

In 2024, our founders were running a digital marketing agency and spending countless hours manually scheduling social media posts across multiple platforms. Frustrated by the lack of intelligent automation tools, they decided to build their own solution.

What started as an internal tool quickly gained interest from other agencies and content creators. Today, AutoPost helps thousands of businesses automate their social media presence while maintaining authenticity and engagement.
